UV Protection & Views

Control The Glare,
Keep The View

Advanced solar fabrics that filter heat and UV rays without darkening your room.

1. Why Choose Sunscreen?

The intelligent choice for living areas and offices.

95% UV Blockage

Protect your expensive hardwood floors, leather furniture, and artwork from fading under the harsh UAE sun, while still enjoying natural light.

Glare Reduction

Specifically designed to cut glare on TV and computer screens. The perfect solution for home offices and media rooms that don't require total darkness.

Daytime Privacy

Enjoy your view during the day while preventing outsiders from seeing in. (Note: At night with lights on inside, the effect reverses).

2. Choose Your Openness

"Openness" refers to how tight the fabric weave is. Lower % means less light.

1%

Maximum Privacy

The tightest weave. Blocks the most heat and glare. The view outside is less distinct (silhouettes).

Best for: High-sun windows
POPULAR
3%

Perfect Balance

The ideal middle ground. Good heat rejection, good glare control, and a clear view of the outside.

Best for: Living Rooms
5%

Maximum View

A looser weave. Lets in more natural light and offers the clearest view, but blocks slightly less heat.

Best for: North-facing windows

Important: Nighttime Privacy

Sunscreen blinds work on a "light balance" principle. During the day, it's brighter outside, so you can see out but no one can see in.

At night, when your lights are on, the effect reverses. People outside can see in. For bedrooms, we recommend pairing Sunscreens with a Blackout Roller or Curtains (Dual System).
