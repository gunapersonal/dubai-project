Smart Home Ready

Effortless Automation

Control your privacy and light with a touch, a tap, or a voice command.

1. Choose Your Power Source

No wiring? No problem. We have solutions for every home.

Wire-Free (Rechargeable)

Perfect for existing homes. These motors feature built-in lithium-ion batteries that only need charging once or twice a year.

Best For: Retrofitting, apartments, and hard-to-reach windows. No electrician required.

  • Clean, wireless install
  • Long-lasting battery life

Hardwired (Mains Power)

Connected directly to your home's electricity. This is a "fit and forget" solution that never requires charging and can handle heavier blinds.

Best For: New builds, renovations, and very large panoramic windows.

  • Zero maintenance
  • Unlimited power for heavy curtains

2. Take Control

Three ways to move your shades.

Remote Control

Simple, tactile control. Operate single blinds or groups of blinds with one button press.

Smartphone App

Control your blinds from anywhere in the world. Set schedules to open at sunrise and close at sunset.

Voice Command

"Hey Google, open the living room blinds." Fully compatible with Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit.

What Can Be Motorized?

Almost everything in our collection. We partner with industry leaders like **Somfy** and **Tuya** to bring automation to:

Roller & Zebra Blinds

Tubular motors hidden inside the roller tube.

Curtains & Drapes

Electric tracks for smooth, silent gliding. Supports heavy drapes.

Child Safe & Cordless

Motorization eliminates dangerous hanging cords, making it the safest choice for homes with children and pets.
