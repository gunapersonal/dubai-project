Soft Furnishings

Tailored Elegance

Add texture, warmth, and acoustic comfort to your home with our premium, made-to-measure curtains.

1. Choose Your Heading Style

The heading defines the fold and character of the drape.

Ripple Fold (Wave)

A modern, continuous wave effect that stacks back neatly. Ideal for floor-to-ceiling windows and motorized tracks.

Pinch Pleat

Classic and tailored. Gathered fabric creates a fuller, more traditional look. Available in Double or Triple pinch.

Eyelet

Contemporary metal rings threaded directly onto a pole. Offers deep, soft folds and a casual finish.

2. Select Your Fabric Opacity

From airy light filtration to complete darkness.

Sheers & Voiles

Lightweight translucent fabrics that soften daylight and provide daytime privacy without blocking the view.

Dimout

Reduces significant light but doesn't create total darkness. Great for living areas where privacy is key.

Blackout Lining

Thick lining added to any face fabric. Blocks 100% of light and reduces heat gain. Essential for bedrooms.

3. Hardware & Automation

The track determines the movement. Choose from decorative poles for a traditional look or sleek motorized tracks for modern convenience.

Motorized Tracks (Somfy/Glydea)

Control your curtains via remote, app, or a gentle pull ("Touch Motion"). Supports heavy drapes effortlessly.

Decorative Poles

Available in wood, metal, and acrylic finishes with various finial designs to match your interior decor.
