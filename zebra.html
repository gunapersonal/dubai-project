The Modern Zebra Blind

The perfect blend of sheer elegance and privacy control in one innovative system.

1. Dual-Layer Ingenuity

Two layers of fabric moving in harmony.

Sliding Stripes

Zebra blinds feature alternating horizontal stripes of **sheer** and **solid** fabric. By adjusting the blind slightly, you slide these layers past each other.

Day Mode (Open)

Align the sheer stripes to let soft, filtered daylight pour into the room while maintaining a view of the outdoors.

Night Mode (Closed)

Align the solid stripes to create a continuous block of fabric for complete privacy and light reduction.

Slide to Adjust Light
