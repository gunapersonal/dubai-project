1. Privacy Policy

At DecoShade, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect personal information (Name, Phone, Email, Location) solely for the purpose of providing quotes, scheduling consultations, and processing orders.

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • Your data is stored securely and only accessed by our staff.
  • You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting info@decorus.ae.

2. Terms of Service

Quotations

All quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue. Prices are subject to change based on final measurements taken by our technical team.

Payment

A 50% deposit is required to commence fabrication of bespoke orders. The remaining 50% balance is due upon completion of installation.

Cancellations

As all our products are made-to-measure, orders cannot be cancelled once fabrication has begun (typically 24 hours after deposit). Deposits are non-refundable after this period.

3. Warranty

We provide a 1-Year Warranty on all fabrics and mechanisms, and a 5-Year Warranty on motorized components. This warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover misuse, accidental damage, or normal wear and tear.
